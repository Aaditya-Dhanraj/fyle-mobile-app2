Log Mileage Using Fyle.
On mobile or web, choose to create a new expense and select 'Log Mileage'. Add your travel end points and we'll automatically calculate mileage and log it.
Capture Email Receipts in One-Click
Just open your email receipt on Outlook or Gmail, and click on the Fyle Button. It's practically Magic.
Manage GoIbibo Bookings with Fyle
GoIbibo users can connect their GoBiz accounts with Fyle to automatically record travel and hotel bookings.
